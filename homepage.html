About

It is an online media player or web service that allows users to stream and play various types of media content saved in the database over the internet. These medias are designed to support a wide range of formats including audio, video, and sometimes even images. Users can access online media players through web browsers or dedicated applications on various devices such as computers, smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and gaming consoles. It required a username and a unique password. It is mandatory to have an account on this platform. This web service is designed by Mr. Aayush Raj Thakur.
